# Simple Tutorials

These programs demonstrate frequently useful openCV functions. They are not too useful on their own, but they support more complex tasks.

* getting started
  * hello.py
  * livecam.py
  * frameRate.py
  * saveVideo.py
* colors and channels
  * bgr.py
  * colorbgr.py
  * hist.py
  * hsv.py
* simple image processing
  * scale.py
  * thresh.py
  * mask.py
  * edge.py
  * feature.py
  * circle.py
